# Reference: Network Scanning

## Nmap Flags

- `-Pn`: Treat all hosts as online (skip ping).
- `-sS`: SYN Scan (Stealth).
- `-p-`: Scan all ports (0-65535).

## PCI Requirement 11.3.4

"If segmentation is used... perform penetration tests at least annually and after any changes... to verify that the segmentation methods are operational and effective."
